1972 Dodge Club Cab

1972 Dodge D100

Even though Dodge set out to build a new pickup every year, it wanted to keep their well known appearance and comfort rather and improving the little things rather than changing the style year after year. Dodge realized that customers didn't necessarily want big changes in vehicles from year to year but more comfortable with traditional proven appearances. Therefore, the Dodge pickups were designed to incorporate more passenger car-like comfort and convenience. The cab was line with double-wall construction to reduce the noise level and which also gave greater strength to the cab and box. There are independent coil spring in the front and leaf springs in the back to carry heavy loads, while the track was widened for stability and a smoother ride. The standard engine was the 225 slant six or the 318, 360 and 400 V8 engines were optional. The Dodge Pickup could be ordered in 3 different trim options - Adventurer, Adventurer sport and Adventurer Special Edition and 2 camper packages were available - Heavy-duty package and trailer-towing package.

The Maixvan featured one of the largest cargo areas along with the widest side door of any van built in the U.S. and came available in a 109 or 127 inch wheelbase. It was equipped with Power disc front brakes, independent front suspension, 26 gallon gas tank, plus more.

1972 Dodge VIN Decoding

VIN Numbers

Serial Numbers were stamped on a plate on the left door lock pillar.

 
First & Second Symbols - Model
B1 - B100 Van M3 - M300
B2 - B200 Van P2 - P200
B3 - B300 Van P3 - P300
D1 - D100 W1 - W100
D2 - D200 W2 - W200
D3 - D300 W3 - W300
G1 - Post Office X3 - WM300 export
Third Symbol - Body
1 - Compact Van; Conventional Cab; LCF Cab; Standard HD Tilt Cab 6 - Compact Low-Line Maxiwagon; Sportsman; Sweptline Crew Cab
2 - Compact Low-Line Wagon; Sportsman; Crew Cab; Long Sleeper HD Tilt Cab 7 - Compact Mid-Line Maxiwagon; Custom Sportsman; Flat-face Cowl
3 - Compact Mid-Line Wagon; Custom Sportsman; Conventional Utiline 8 - Compact Hi-Line Maxiwagon; Royal Sportsman; Windshield Cowl
4 - Compact Hi-Line Wagon; Royal Sportsman; Conventional Sweptline 9- Forward Control or Motorhome; Incomplete vehicle
5 - Compact Maxivan; Tradesman; Utiline Crew Cab  
Fourth Symbol - Gross Volume Weight (GVW)
A - 6,000 lbs or less C - 10,001 to 14,000 lbs
B - 6,001 to 10,000 lbs D - 14,001 to 16,000 lbs
Fifth Symbol - Engine Type
B - 225-1 F - 360
C - 225-2 G - LA318-3
E - LA318-1 J- 400
Sixth Symbol - Model Year
2 = 1972
Seventh Symbol - Assembly Plant
J - Windsor, Ontario, Canada T - Warren Truck No. 2
N- Burt Road, Detroit, Michigan U - St. Louis, Missouri
S - Warren Truck No. 1 V - Warren
Eighth Symbol - Production Sequence Number
Sequence numbers began with 500001
